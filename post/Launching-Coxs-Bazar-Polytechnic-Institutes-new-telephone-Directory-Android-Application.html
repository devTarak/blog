Cox's Bazar Polytechnic Institute Launches New Telephone Directory Application

Cox's Bazar Polytechnic Institute (CBPI) has launched a new telephone directory application to help students, faculty, and staff easily find the contact information they need. The application, which is available for free on the Link bellow, features a searchable database of all CBPI Teachers and staffs phone numbers, including departmental extensions, faculty offices, and student dormitories.

"We are excited to launch this new telephone directory application," said CBPI Principal Mohsinur Rahman. "We believe that this will be a valuable resource for our community and will help to make communication on campus more efficient.

The application was developed by Tarak Rahman. The application is a valuable resource for students, faculty, and staff at CBPI. It makes it easy to find the contact information they need and helps to improve communication on campus.
